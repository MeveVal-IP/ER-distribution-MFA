Class-level ER distributions with genus stratification

Within each eligible class (those with at least 100 ERs in our dataset), up to five representative genera are shown in color. These genera are selected based on the presence of large ERs. For every class, we provide both linear (%chr) and log-scaled (log₁₀(%chr)) versions of the ΔGC vs. %chr plots.

All plots are fully interactive, allowing detailed exploration of genus-level distributions. Together, these figures extend the examples shown in the main text and offer a deeper view of replicon diversity and evolutionary patterns across bacterial taxa.
